Tips & Tricks

Get better results

💡

Use High Contrast

Images where the subject clearly differs from the background give the best results. Avoid busy or patterned backgrounds when possible.

📐

Optimal Resolution

Images between 500px and 4000px wide work best. Very small images may lose fine details like hair strands or thin edges.

🎨

Product Photography

Place products on a plain white or solid-color backdrop for near-perfect automated cutouts every time, ready for e-commerce.
